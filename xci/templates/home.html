This app demonstrates one way to connect existing technologies to make smarter and more tailored tools for students. The goals are:

  • Show how a system can use the competency URIs to find the machine readable version of a competency.
  • Show how a competency URI can act as the object of some experience, or as part of the context of an experience.
  • Show how content relevant to a learners' goals can become discoverable in the Learning Registry (LR) by associating the competency URI to the content in the LR.

Getting Started

Log in or Sign up from the nav bar at the top of the page.

After logging in, explore the competencies and sets. Sign up for a competency. Search the LR and associate comptencies to the LR results.
